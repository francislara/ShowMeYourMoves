Connect with nearby smash players!

Show Me Your Moves allows you to find nearby Super Smash Bros players to connect and play with! No more netplay lag, no more connection issues. Just real Smash with real people.

Find Out More

We've got what you need!

Show Me Your Moves has everything you need to start playing Super Smash Bros with other people near you. Broadcast your general location in the map, or find other players or events nearby.

Get Started!

Created with the following technologies

Expo SDK

Expo SDK allows for easy login through Google and Facebook.

React Native

React Native makes the app feel great on Android and iOS.

Ruby on Rails

Ruby on Rails for our backend keeps things simple and versatile, allowing new features to be easily added.

Google Maps API

Our maps use the Google Maps API, making it easy to get started and familiar to use.
